Question:

Can we predict drug use using basic, legally accessible information about a job candidate?

Drug User:

  • Used any major illicit drug in previous 12 months

Classification Model: Cost Considerations

1. Mis-labeled as a drug-user (FP):

  • Extended search costs
  • Lost productivity due to missing worker
  • Decreased reputation due to errant rejections

2. Accept drug-user into applicant pool (FN):

  • Waste employee time on interviews
  • Must restart search after failed drug test
  • If pass drug test, higher risk of subsequent problems

Assumed here:

  • FN 1.25x as expensive as FP

Model Results

Random Forest Classifier

  • 4.2% improvement over baseline
  • 82% accuracy
  • 0.46 F1 - 65% AUC

Feature Importance:

  • Weight
  • # days binged alcohol
  • Age
  • Cigarette use
  • Alcohol use
  • # days unable to work
  • Tendency towards risky behavior
